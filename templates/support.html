MedTrack Support Center

If you need help, you're in the right place. Browse topics or contact us anytime.

Common Questions

  • 💊 How do I add a new medication?
    Go to the Medications tab and click “Add Medication.” Fill in the details and save.
  • 🔔 How can I set reminders?
    Navigate to the Reminders section and choose your schedule and medicine.
  • 📅 How can I book an appointment?
    Click “Book New Appointment” and choose an available doctor and time slot.
